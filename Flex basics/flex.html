About Alagappa University

Alagappa University, located in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, is a premier institution established to foster research, higher education, and extension activities. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India, the university offers a wide variety of programs at the Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral levels across multiple disciplines.

With a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, the university emphasizes innovative teaching methods, industry partnerships, and community engagement. Alagappa University has consistently achieved high rankings and accreditations, making it one of the most sought-after destinations for higher learning in India.
